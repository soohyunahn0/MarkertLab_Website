Freshman Research Initiative



Magnetic Matter

We focus on materials physics and the design and development of materials for use in data storage, optics, and sensors. Students will learn new materials synthesis via solid state reaction, followed by structural, microscopic, magnetic, thermal, and superconducting characterization of the materials. Students may also perform cantilever micromagnetometry, interferometry, and magnetic resonance microscopy.
